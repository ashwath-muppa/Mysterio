Mysterio

Instructions

Follow these steps to play the game:

  • Read the goal and understand the objective.
  • Input your guesses based on the silhouette.
  • Check your score after each guess.
  • If your score falls to zero or below, the game ends.
  • Try to get the highest score to beat your best scores on the leaderboard

Goal

Try and figure out the mystery image! Keep solving until you get a lot wrong.

Input

On the screen will be the silhouette of a mystery item. You are trying to find out what that item is. Every time you guess a word, we will tell you how "far" you are from the answer.

Scoring

Every time you guess, a certain amount of points will be deducted from your possible earnings for the round, based on how close/far you are from the answer. Once the round ends, the amount of remaining points will be added to your total score.

Game End

If your possible score for one round ends up falling to or below 0, your game is over. If you score high enough, you can make it on your personal leaderboard!
